# ClipCycle

## Vision

ClipCycle is an AI-powered Content Idea Vault for student creators.

Problem:
Student creators constantly lose good content ideas because they save them across Notes, WhatsApp, screenshots, Instagram saves, bookmarks, and voice notes.

Solution:
Capture ideas in one place.
AI understands them.
AI organizes them.
AI helps convert them into publish-ready content.

Target Users

- College creators
- Instagram creators
- YouTubers
- LinkedIn creators
- Study influencers
